The GeoFlex Stretch Tent

The GeoFlex event tent canopy fabric is reinforced at it’s stress points and made from high quality, fire rated, UV protected, two way stretch fabric with a PVC coating. GeoFlex canopy seams are high frequency welded, making them 100% waterproof and incredibly durable.
